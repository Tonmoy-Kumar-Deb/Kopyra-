Service & Refund Policy

This application follows strict service handling rules. By using our platform you agree that all bookings, orders, and service requests are processed under controlled workflow conditions.

We maintain transparency in all transactions, but certain service conditions apply depending on completion stage, timing, and execution quality.

  • Only verified token-based requests are accepted.
  • Service completion depends on workload and availability.
  • Delays may occur due to system or operational reasons.
  • Refund eligibility is calculated based on service progress.

If any work is partially completed, only a limited refund portion is applicable. In most cases, up to 40% refund may be provided depending on evaluation of work progress and execution stage.

If the service is fully completed or delivered, refund requests may not be accepted except in exceptional system error cases.

We reserve the right to modify, delay, or partially complete any service if technical or operational constraints occur.

By proceeding further, you agree that service outcomes are dependent on system availability and processing conditions.
